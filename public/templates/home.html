About Plaster

The Plaster web app helps you compare different operations at different hospitals based on price and quality. Super handy if you need to get a brain surgery and are on a tight budget!

Plaster was born at the AngelHack Hospital. The birth lasted less than 24h and was assisted by and data surgeon Tele Hao and code doctor Anders Rex.
